About The Game

DYSMANTLE - Post-Apocalyptic Survival RPG

DYSMANTLE is an open-world action RPG with survival elements set in a post-apocalyptic world. After escaping from a long-lasting underground shelter, you find yourself in a strange new world full of dangerous creatures, mysterious ruins, and scattered survivors.

In DYSMANTLE you can break over 99% of all objects for materials, craft permanent weapons, tools, outfits, and trinkets, hunt dangerous creatures or tame them for your post-apocalyptic zoo ranch, solve puzzles above ground and underground in the Tombs of the Old Ones, and build outposts to establish your presence in this unforgiving world.
