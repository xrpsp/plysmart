About The Game

Persona 5 The Phantom X is the latest installment in the acclaimed Persona series, bringing the iconic style and thrilling gameplay to mobile devices. Embark on an epic journey as a high school student who becomes a Phantom Thief, navigating between the realities of daily life and the supernatural Metaverse.

With stunning visuals, a captivating story, and a memorable cast of characters, Persona 5 The Phantom X delivers an unparalleled RPG experience. Battle shadows, form friendships, and unleash your Persona in this beautifully crafted adventure.
